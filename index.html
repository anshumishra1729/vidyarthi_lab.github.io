About Vidyarthi Lab

Vidyarthi Lab is a spiritual study group formed by students of IISER Berhampur who share a common interest in exploring the depths of Vedanta and other schools of Indian philosophy.

Under the compassionate guidance of Swami Shyamananda Ji, we gather regularly to study sacred texts, discuss philosophical concepts, and apply these timeless teachings in our daily lives as students and seekers.

" Life is not a hundred meter sprint but a marathon to run. One short failure doent mean life is over." - Swami Shyamananda Ji

Our Activities

At Vidyarthi Lab, we engage in various activities to nurture both intellectual understanding and personal transformation:

Scripture Study

Daily study sessions on Bhagavad Gita, Upanishads, and other foundational texts of Vedanta.

Satsang

Weekly Sunday Satsang and Q&A sessions with Swami Shyamananda Ji and visiting scholars.

Meditation Practice

Guided meditation sessions to develop inner stillness and self-awareness.

Reflective Writing

Opportunities to journal and share personal insights from our spiritual journey.

Student Reflections

" I wish to express my gratitude to our Swamiji who helped me reinforce myself and apply to foreign universities. Back in March 2024, I was in a state of mental exhaustion and because of some family problems, I thought of giving up the idea of going to foreign and doing a PhD. However, Swamiji from the beginning of our personal interactions, helped me understand how I can tackle my personal problems and let them not be a barrier to my progress. He helped me think in the long term and not get narrowed down by some present problems. I was having a mild state of depression near the end of year 2024. This world of intense material activity and competition seemed very odd to me. However, Swamiji has given time to me whenever I wanted to have a discussion with him on some personal or philosophical matters. Swamiji has also helped in understanding the science of Pranayama over some discussion sessions. Pranam to our Swamiji.."

- Amitabh Roy
