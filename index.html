About Vidyarthi Lab

Vidyarthi Lab is a spiritual study group formed by students of IISER Berhampur who share a common interest in exploring the depths of Vedanta and other schools of Indian philosophy.

Under the compassionate guidance of Swami Shyamananda Ji, we gather regularly to study sacred texts, discuss philosophical concepts, and apply these timeless teachings in our daily lives as students and seekers.

"Knowledge is not just to be acquired but to be lived. The laboratory for this living is your own life." - Swami Shyamananda Ji

Our Activities

At Vidyarthi Lab, we engage in various activities to nurture both intellectual understanding and personal transformation:

Scripture Study

Weekly study sessions on Bhagavad Gita, Upanishads, and other foundational texts of Vedanta.

Satsang

Group discussions and Q&A sessions with Swami Shyamananda Ji and visiting scholars.

Meditation Practice

Guided meditation sessions to develop inner stillness and self-awareness.

Reflective Writing

Opportunities to journal and share personal insights from our spiritual journey.
